Who We Are

A Table Worth Returning To

Founded in 2018, Olive & Flame has become one of the city's favorite dining destinations, offering premium ingredients and exceptional service.

What started as a small neighborhood bistro has grown into a celebrated culinary landmark. Our kitchen is guided by a simple belief: great food begins with great ingredients, prepared by people who truly care.

Every dish on our menu is a reflection of that philosophy — seasonal, sourced with intention, and crafted to create moments that linger long after the last bite.
